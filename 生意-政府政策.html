Jinwan District

In order to further strengthen the efforts to support enterprises to go public and to encourage and promote more enterprises to take advantage of the new opportunities of capital market reform to achieve leap-forward development, on January 31, 2022, Zhuhai Jinwan District Financial Service Center and Zhuhai Jinwan District Finance Bureau jointly issued the Implementation Measures for Promoting Enterprises to Take Advantage of Capital Market Development (hereinafter referred to as the Implementation Measures). These Measures shall come into force on March 3, 2022 and shall be valid for three years.
