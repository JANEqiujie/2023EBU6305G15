Overview of Zhuhai Economy
In 2021, the city's GDP reached 388.175 billion yuan, with a year-on-year growth of 6.9% (the same below)
Per capita GDP was 157,900 yuan, up by 4.0 percent
The added value of industries above designated size was 133.937 billion yuan, up by 8.8% The investment in fixed assets was 216.223 billion yuan, down by 3.1% The total retail sales of consumer goods reached 104.824 billion yuan, up by 13.8%
The total value of imports and exports was 332.008 billion yuan, up by 21.5%
Actual foreign direct investment was 2.898 billion US dollars, up by 13.4% Revenue in the general public budget is 44.819 billion yuan, up 18.2%
